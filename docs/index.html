Questions

What kind of signals does Scoop detect?

Executive changes, funding rounds, M&A activity, layoffs, product launches, regulatory actions, strategic pivots, and earnings surprises. We scan the entire web and filter to what's relevant to your deals.

How is this different from Google Alerts?

Google Alerts sends you raw links. Scoop gives you a synthesized brief with context on why each signal matters to your specific deal. It's the difference between a news feed and an intelligence analyst.

What if my accounts are small or private companies?

Scoop works best with companies that have some public footprint. For smaller accounts, we surface signals from industry publications, job postings, social media, and regulatory filings that larger tools miss.

Do I need to install anything?

No. Scoop is email-only. No app, no browser extension, no CRM plugin. It shows up in your inbox every Monday morning.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Reply "stop" to any digest and you're out. No contracts, no commitments.
